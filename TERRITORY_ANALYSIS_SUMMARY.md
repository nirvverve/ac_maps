   - Clear geographic performance patterns indicate operational factors

2. **Account Density Matters**
   - East: 15.83 accounts/zip ‚Üí 23% retention
   - West: 11.76 accounts/zip ‚Üí 13% retention
   - Higher density correlates with better retention
